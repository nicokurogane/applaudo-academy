Code for a change.

We believe teaching code can truly change lives. For us, this is the best way to help solve the immigration crisis here at home.

We are partnering up with Kodigo Foundation to look for the best talent in the country. Our goal is to teach each trainee the standards of Software Development and place them into distributed teams of great talent for world-class customers.

  • Applaudo Studios will donate 10% of revenue to the program.
  • We create high-paying jobs in tech.
  • We create long term jobs with low turnover
  • We will create zero student debt.
  • We empower families to increase their incomes by $15,000+
  • We provide high-quality healthcare insurance

Let’s build stability here at home and keep the talent in El Salvador!
